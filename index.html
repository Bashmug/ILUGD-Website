About us

We are linux users from Delhi NCR region. We mostly include students and professionals who uses linux. We believe in endorsing OSS/FOSS and linux use and development of the same. As of now we organise monthly meetup. We respect all technologies from Perl to Python. If you want to volunteer for ILUGD you can join us in any of our social channels.
